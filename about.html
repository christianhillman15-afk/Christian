A bit of background

I've spent my career helping small and mid-sized businesses show up online — with websites that actually convert and marketing that actually reaches people. The common thread is taking something that feels complicated and turning it into a simple, working growth engine.

Today I work on both sides of that equation. With Oxsome, I help businesses get online and grow through custom subscription websites and AI-driven marketing. With Launch Media, I help brands reach the right audiences through programmatic advertising across display, connected TV, and digital audio.

That mix means I can look at your whole picture — not just the website, and not just the ads, but how the two work together to bring in customers.
